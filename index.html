Our Mission

We Exist to Build Bridges Between Possibility and Reality

HopeBridge Foundation believes that every person deserves the fundamental dignities of education, clean water, healthcare, and opportunity — regardless of where they were born. Since 2009, we have worked in 25 countries to make that belief a lived reality.

Our approach is community-led: we listen first, then act alongside local partners to create programs that endure long after we leave.

Integrity

We hold ourselves accountable to our donors and communities with full transparency.

Sustainability

We design programs that communities can own and grow independently.

Community First

Local voices drive every decision we make.

Innovation

We seek smarter ways to solve persistent human challenges.

FAQ

Questions Answered

How is my donation used?
93 cents of every dollar goes directly to our programs. The remaining 7% covers essential fundraising and administrative costs. We publish full financial statements annually and are audited by an independent firm.
Is my donation tax-deductible?
Yes. HopeBridge Foundation is a registered 501(c)(3) in the United States and holds equivalent charity status in the UK, Canada, and Australia. Donors receive a receipt automatically for tax purposes.
Can I see where my donation goes?
Absolutely. Monthly donors receive impact updates including photos and stories from the field. All donors can access our real-time spending dashboard via their donor portal.
How do I become a volunteer?
Click "Volunteer" in our navigation menu or use the form on this page. We accept volunteers for field missions (minimum 2-week commitment), remote support, and local event organizing.
Do you accept corporate partnerships?
Yes, and we actively seek them. Corporate partnerships can take many forms: donation matching, cause-related marketing, employee volunteer programs, and direct project sponsorship. Contact us to explore options.
Can I cancel a monthly donation?
Yes, at any time with no fees or penalties. Log into your donor portal or email us at donors@hopebridge.org and we'll process your request within one business day.
